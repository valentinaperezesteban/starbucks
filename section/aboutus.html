About Us

Starbucks opened its doors, for the first time, as the days went by in March 1971. Those first coffee beans, among an intense variety of teas and spices of the world were found in those four walls to offer each customer an invitation to take part from that world -their world- to their homes. And especially, share it.

The Starbucks.

In 1987, the color green replaced the brown of the aprons to build a reference forever. Soon Starbucks expanded to Chicago, Vancouver (Canada), California, Washington, and New York. In 1996 he crossed the Pacific and landed first in Japan, then in Europe two years later and in China in 1999. The connection was global.

Starbucks Company

We are an international chain of coffee shops, dedicated to the largest coffee trade in the world, with more than 24,000 stores in 70 countries.
